I'm an enthusiastic & detail-oriented Robotics Researcher.


Passionate, Highly Motivated Mechanical & Computing Engineer.
Having a Bias for Action, Breaking down and Tackling complex problems efficiently.


Research Interests: Particles Manipulation, VLM, Tool Affordance, Multi-Robot Systems, Perceptual Robots, and Automation.


About Me


Currently, I am a postdoctoral fellow and a lab manager in the Robotic and Machine Intelligence Lab (ROMI-Lab) at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), under the guidance of Dr. David Navarro-Alarcon.


Prior to this, I was a PhD student in the ROMI-Lab at PolyU, where I successfully defended my doctoral dissertation in Mechanical Engineering in 2024.


Before that, I had the opportunity to work as a visiting scholar at the Bristol Robotics Laboratory in the United Kingdom, working under the supervision of Professor Chenguang (Charlie) Yang from the University of Liverpool.
